About Pidan Workshop

Who We Are

Pidan Workshop is a creative studio focused on developing innovative games and digital products. We believe in the power of creativity, technology, and community to create meaningful experiences.

What We Do

  • Game Development: We create engaging HTML5 games using modern game engines like Defold
  • Product Design: We build innovative tools and solutions for creative professionals
  • Community Building: We share knowledge and collaborate with developers worldwide

Our Mission

To create games and products that inspire creativity, spark joy, and bring people together through innovative technology and thoughtful design.
